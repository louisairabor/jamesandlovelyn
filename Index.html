Our Story

About James & Lovelyn Nig. Ltd.

Founded in 2018 in Benin City, Edo State, James & Lovelyn Nigeria Limited was born from a shared vision — to bring premium, sustainably sourced palm products to Nigerian and global markets with integrity, consistency, and care.

What started as a modest palm produce trading operation has grown into a full-cycle palm product enterprise, handling everything from plantation sourcing and primary processing to packaging and international export. Today, we supply palm oil, palm kernel nuts, palm kernel cake, palm kernel shells, crude palm sludge, and more — serving buyers across Africa, Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

At the heart of our business is a deep commitment to quality assurance and environmental responsibility. Every product that leaves our facility passes through rigorous inspection, and every sourcing relationship we maintain is built on ethical and transparent practices.

We are more than a supplier — we are a dependable, long-term partner for businesses that demand reliability, traceability, and excellence in every shipment.

"We built this company on trust. Our word is our bond — when we commit to a shipment, a quality, or a price, we deliver." — Founder, James & Lovelyn Agabi.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred supplier of premium palm and kernel products in West Africa and across global markets — renowned for our uncompromising quality, sustainable practices, and commitment to transforming Nigeria's agricultural export potential.

We envision a future where Nigerian palm products are synonymous with world-class excellence, traceable sourcing, and responsible industry leadership.

Our Mission

To consistently source, process, and deliver the highest quality palm and kernel products while upholding sustainable agricultural practices, creating value for our farmers, clients, and communities.

We achieve this through rigorous quality controls, transparent business relationships, responsible environmental stewardship, and a relentless drive to exceed the expectations of every buyer and partner we serve.
